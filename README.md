# LVN Industries / Lovan Vehicle Network

Welcome to the official digital repository for **LVN Industries**, operating locally on Vancouver Island as **Lovan Mechanical Services**. We provide enterprise-grade, high-performance mobile mechanical diagnostics, maintenance, and emergency field repair services for commercial transport fleets, heavy equipment, and industrial small tools.

Our long-term blueprint integrates modern trade diagnostics, equipment lifecycle management, and asset networks into a unified service-sales-rental ecosystem.

---

## 🛠️ Core Capabilities & Specializations

* **Advanced Systems Diagnostics:** Full-spectrum electronic control wiring, sensor tracing, and heavy machinery fault isolation.
* **Heavy-Duty Fleet Maintenance:** Commercial transport powertrain overhauls, preventative maintenance optimization, and logging/construction support.
* **Small Tool Utility & Repair:** Fast-turnaround diagnostics, mechanical overhaul, and precision repair for industrial small tools, generators, and compact equipment (**Active & Available Now**).
* **Uptime Infrastructure:** Custom-engineered mobile field response designed strictly to minimize fleet and project downtime.
